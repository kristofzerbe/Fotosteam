{{> SetupProvider}}

FOTOSTEAM interacts with Dropbox as a so-called Dropbox-App, i.e. we have our own folder named Fotosteam under the folder Apps, where we will store your optimized photos. We have only access to this folder.

Most easiest way to get your photos on your FOTOSTEAM page is, if you copy them to your local folder .../Dropbox/App/Fotosteam/ToPublish, wait until your Dropbox client has uploaded all files and then click on the button Synchronize on your dashboard.

With Dropbox you have furthermore the possibility to enable automatic synchronization of your ToPublishfolder. Thereby Dropbox is notifying our server, if a new file is present and we will process it automatically. For that you find a switch in your dashboard options.

Important:
For linking correctly to your provider, please beware of your browsers popup windows and allow them for this site!
In case it went wrong, delete your account and create a new one.

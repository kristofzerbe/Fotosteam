How FOTOSTEAM works

First of all...

Your data stay where they belong: with you!

FOTOSTEAM, saves your photos at your cloud provider and not on a server, where you don't have direct access. This way, you keep full and easy control over your data!

Step 1

Create your FOTOSTEAM account, by registering up via one of the following social media services, because, neither we store your password...

{{> StartAuthBrand}}

Step 2

You connect your account with one of the available cloud providers...

{{> StartCloudBrand}}

Step 3

You upload your photos either via this website or you just copy them in the folder named .../Fotosteam/ToPublish on your hard-drive, which was created during the connect with your cloud provider.

Step 4

Synchronize the photos via the button on your Dashboard. Dropbox users can save the effort by activating automatic sync in their options.

The images will be read from the ToPublish-folder, optimized and stored in several sizes in a new folder under .../Fotosteam/Internal/Fotos, for giving your friends the best photo experience.

Step 5

After the synchronization you will find your new photos on the dashboard tab New Photos.

Done!
